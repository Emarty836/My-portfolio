Bodyweight 1RM Max Calculator

I've been lifting weights for at least 10 years. Overtime, I would use a regular 1 rep max calculator to measure the appropriate next incremental load. The online calculators is striaght forward unless your doing bodyweight exercises. Factoring bodyweight and additional weight with reps made number crunching more tedious than it needed to be. This was my inspiration for this project and its definetely a time saver for me each time I workout. Mid August of 2021 is when I made my first post on github.
